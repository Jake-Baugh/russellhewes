Who we are

Russell Hewes Ltd has been successfully delivering electrical Projects across London for ten years.

With the capacity and experience for large scale Projects, as well as a client-focussed approach and fine attention to detail, our team of London’s leading NICEIC approved electric contractors cover all aspects of domestic and commercial trade work.

At Russell Hewes Ltd we are known for efficiency and honesty, with a long history of happy clients and plentiful testimonials available on request.

Whether it’s a complete property development, maintenance or an urgent repair, contact us for a bespoke quote for your next project.

We are covered by the NICEIC platinum promise which guarantees our clients the highest standard of work and commitment.
