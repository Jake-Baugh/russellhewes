who we are

Russell Hewes Electicians Ltd has grown to become one of Londons leading electrical contractors.

We are trusted on a daily basis to work in the homes and work places of other people and this gives us the platform to provide the quality of work and service that we would expect in our own homes.

We offer repair maintenance and project development services to both commercial and domestic clients, and our experienced team has extensive knowledge in all aspects of building and management, allowing us to take on large scale projects but also cover reactive maintenance.
