Our electrical team worked on a beautiful Georgian home located in a tranquil cobbled Mews, just off of Chester Row in Victoria, London.

  • Site Residential
  • Location Victoria, London
  • Scope Part Rewire, Installation, AV & Leutron
  • Type Electrical
  • Status Complete

Work carried out includes a partial rewire as well as an AV and Leutron overhaul. We installed new fixtures and fittings to replace the old worn out ones which were previously in place in the bathroom, kitchen, reception areas and sauna - Incorporating georgous brass finish to match the clean, contemporary aesthetic of the newly refurbished home. Our team test and approve the property so that the client can be confident in knowing that their home is approved and certified before moving in.
