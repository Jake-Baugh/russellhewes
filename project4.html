Our electrical team worked on a quirky studio flat based in Oval, London.

  • Site Residential
  • Location Clapham, London
  • Scope Full Rewire, Power Installation, Specialist LED Fittings
  • Type Electrical
  • Status Complete

The work carried out included a full rewire, the installation of power to the kitchen as well as bespoke LED lighting for the kitchen and bathroom areas. The overall aim of the project was to create an open concept living space, using the balance of natural and artificial light to open up the available space.
